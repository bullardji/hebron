About the Program

The Hebron Collegium is a residential discipleship program focused on the study of Scripture, prayer, and communal life. We provide a place for young Christian men to grow in wisdom and stature through daily routines of worship and service.

Located in Rockford, Illinois, the house fosters brotherhood and accountability while equipping men for lives of faithful vocation in the Church and world.
